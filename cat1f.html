Tempr Sensors & Thermowell - Thermowell Threaded

Description

Thermo-wells are among the simplest yet least well publicized accessories used in industrial temperature measurement applications. There are many variations of two basic kinds; low pressure and high pressure. They are used to provide an isolation between a temperature sensor and the environment, either liquid, gas or slurry. A thermo-well allows the temperature sensor to be removed and replaced without compromising either the ambient region or the process.

Various types of Thermo-wells are :
  • - Threaded Type Thermo-well.
  • - Flanged Type Thermo-well.
  • - Limited Space Thermo-well.
  • - Weld-In Type Thermo-well.
  • - Stock Weld Type Thermo-well.
