Temperature Scanner - 'TemSCAN'

Description

We, at Surya Instruments, manufacture Temperature Scanners with required number of channels as per our customer needs along with soft features for temperature scanning at different locations at an instantaneous time. To produce highly reliable and standard quality of temperature scanners, we are well equipped and supported with experts enlightened with latest technology of equipment features.

Temperature Scanner 'TemSCAN' - Features :
  • - Microcontroller based unit.
  • - We customize various type temperaure scanner viz. 4, 8, 12, 16 & 24 channel
  • - Solid State Scanning.
  • - Every channel has it's own signal conditioning and calibration.
  • - Auto / Manual scanning selection.
  • - Skip option for each channel.
  • - Easy front panel soft key calibration facility.
  • - RS-485 / RS232 communication interface.
  • - Maximum of 4 set points with alarm relays.
  • - Custom Built Features on request.
Temperature Scanner 'TemSCAN' - Applications :
  • - Ovens & Furnaces in heat treatment plants and laboratories.
  • - Process industries and Engineering Industries.
  • - Diesel Generator Sets (DG Sets)
  • - Turbines for bearing temperatures.
  • - Pharmaceuticals.
  • - College Projects and R & D Centres.
  • - Data Acquisition Systems
