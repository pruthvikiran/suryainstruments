Tempr Sensors & Thermowell - J type Big Omega

Description

We are the manufacturers in INDIA for all types of RTD’s and thermo-couples, can provide up to a maximum length of 1000mm. Main raw material is procured from Switzerland based world class supplier and the sensors are named for their low self heating rate, excellent long term stability, resistant against vibrations & temperature shocks etc. Various types of inspections are conducted throughout the process including dye penetration and hydrostatic tests to ensure the quality, reliability and integrity of our thermo-couples. Can be provided in various grades of stainless steel, non-ferrous and a variety of high temperature super alloys.

We are open for welcoming suggestions for improvement and cooperation in area of production of modern and reliable thermo-couples and accessories. We hope that our products will attract customer’s interest and we wish to prove our mettle in satisfying customer’s needs.
